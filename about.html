About Sarah Banwart

Retired therapist turned trading psychology expert, helping traders master their mental edge through evidence-based psychological strategies.

"Trading success begins in the mind"

From Therapy Room to Trading Floor

With over 15 years of experience as a licensed clinical social worker (MSW), I've dedicated my career to understanding the human mind and helping people overcome their psychological barriers. My transition into trading psychology was a natural evolution - recognizing that the same emotional patterns that affect personal relationships and life decisions also impact trading performance.

Professional Background

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (MSW) - 15+ years practice
  • Trading Psychology Specialist - 5+ years focused on traders
  • Certified Cognitive Behavioral Therapist
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction Instructor

My Mission

I believe that successful trading is 80% psychology and 20% strategy. My mission is to help traders develop the mental resilience, emotional discipline, and psychological edge needed to navigate the markets successfully. Through Mental Edge Trading, I provide practical, evidence-based tools that address the real psychological challenges traders face daily.

What I Help With

Overcoming revenge trading and emotional decision-making

Managing FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) and impulsive trades

Building discipline and consistent trading habits

Developing resilience after losses
