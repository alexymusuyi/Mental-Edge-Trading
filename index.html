Meet Sarah

A retired therapist who became a trader and discovered just how brutal the emotional side of the markets can be. Now she helps traders build structure, resilience, and self-trust.

Retired therapist → Trader

Sarah Banwart, MSW, has spent years helping people navigate anxiety, burnout, and high-pressure decision making. When she stepped into trading, she saw the same patterns show up: fight, flight, freeze, and shame after losses.

Mental Edge Trading exists to close that gap. Sarah blends her theraputic and psychological experiance with her trading experience so you can learn to think like a professional trader by mastering your mind.

On the About page, you’ll get the full story, how she trades now, and the philosophy behind everything she teaches.
